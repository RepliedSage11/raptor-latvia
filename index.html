What is Raptor?
Two-component, high-strength urethane protective coating against rust, corrosion, salt, mildew and extreme temperatures. RAPTOR restores the most worn surfaces of pickups and trucks. Forms high strength surface resistant to mechanical damage and ultraviolet radiation. Possesses high adhesion, fast drying time, water-repellent and soundproofing properties.
